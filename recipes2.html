Recipes 2 Prawn caesar salad

Combined elements of a caesar salad and a prawn cocktail to create this deliciously fresh, summery dish.

Serves 4

  • 4 eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 Complete Caesar Salad mix
  • 600g medium cooked prawns, peeled leaving tails intact, deveined
  • 1 avocado, halved, stone removed, peeled, coarsely chopped

Step 1

Place the eggs Unsure of the quantity needed? Click on the underlined ingredient to reveal the quantity. No need to flip back and forth! OK, GOT IT in a small saucepan of cold water. Bring to the boil over high heat. Reduce heat to medium and gently boil, stirring occasionally, for 4-5 minutes for a soft yolk or until cooked to your liking. Drain and cool under cold running water. Peel and cut into quarters.

Step 2

Place the salad mix in a bowl, reserving the dressing. Add prawns to the salad mix. Toss to combine.

Step 3

Divide the egg, salad mixture and avocado among serving bowls.

Step 4

Drizzle over the reserved dressing.
