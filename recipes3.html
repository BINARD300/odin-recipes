Recipes 3 Crispy-skinned salmon

The secret of crispy-skinned salmon. With our clever tips you won't have to go out for dinner!

Feeds four people

  • 4 (200g each) salmon fillets, skin on
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt flakes
  • Steamed chat potatoes, to serve
  • Salad leaves, to serve

Step 1

Place salmon Unsure of the quantity needed? Click on the underlined ingredient to reveal the quantity. No need to flip back and forth! OK, GOT IT , skin side up, on a plate. Drizzle with oil. Rub salt into skin.

Step 2

Heat a large, non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat. Add salmon, skin side down. Cook for 4 to 5 minutes or until skin is crisp. Turn. Cook, covered for 4 to 5 minutes for medium or until cooked to your liking.

Step 3

Serve with potatoes and salad leaves.
